Corpus Analysis Project

Background

I will be comparing an contrasting the Inagural Adresses and State of The Union speeches between the first president, George Washington. Against the most recent and current president, Donald Trump. I thin that It will be interesring to see now the language and words they use has changed over time.



Word Cloud

George Wasnington tends to use words like constitution and Britain more than Trump as he was ths first president of the United States and was stating his plan for the young, new country. Donald Trump, seemed to use words like America, money and mentioning the two political parties, Democrats and Republicans a lot more than Washington as he is the most recent and current president and is talking on a global scale.

N-Grams

Both of these presidents refer to the United States at a higher frequency than any other 3 bit N-Gram. Trump refering to it less than Washington makes sense to me, as he was the first president and Trump was the 45th and 47th.
